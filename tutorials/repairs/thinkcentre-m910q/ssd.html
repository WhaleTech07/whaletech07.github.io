Lenovo ThinkCentre M910q SSD Upgrade

Follow these simple steps to upgrade the SSD in your Lenovo ThinkCentre M910q.

Step 1

Remove the single screw from the rear of the system.

Step 2

Slide the cover toward the front, and lift it from the system.

Step 3

Flip the system over, and slide the bottom cover towards the rear of the system.

Step 4

Use a pair of tweezers to lift out the plastic pin from the SSD.

Step 5

Remove the SSD from the slot.

Step 6

Install the new SSD into the slot.

Step 7

Press the drive down, and insert the plastic pin to secure it.

Step 8

Follow the instructions in reverse for re-assembly.
